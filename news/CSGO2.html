CS:GO2 announced

Counter-Strike 2 is official, and for some, it’s coming today. In a post on Twitter, Valve writes that a limited test for CS2 is starting today, which it describes as an “overhaul to every system, every piece of content, and every part of the C-S experience.” A website for the game describes it as “the largest technical leap forward in Counter-Strike’s history” and promises years of updates and new features..

The full game is expected to release in summer 2023, according to the game’s FAQ. It will be free to play.

CS2 is based on Valve’s Source 2 engine and includes updates to some of the game’s core mechanics, including improved smoke grenades and “sub-tick updates” that Valve says will let “servers know the exact instant that motion starts, a shot is fired, or a ‘nade is thrown.” (A video also appears to show someone clearing grenade smoke with another grenade.) The game will also have revamped maps, better visuals, UI enhancements, reworked audio, and more.

S1mple leads his team to victory in 3 consecutive tournaments

In a remarkable and unprecedented feat, Ukrainian CS:GO prodigy Aleksandr "S1mple" Kostyliev has propelled his team, TBW, to a triumphant streak, securing victory in three consecutive tournaments. S1mple's exceptional skill and masterful leadership were instrumental in the team's success in these highly competitive events.

In the first tournament, S1mple showcased his outstanding ability by leading Team TBW with spectacular and standout plays, effortlessly overcoming opponents. His game-reading prowess and real-time strategic decision-making earned praise from teammates and fans alike.

During the second tournament, S1mple not only excelled in individual fragging but also displayed exceptional tactical leadership. Coordinating his team masterfully, he implemented innovative strategies that left opponents bewildered and led TBW to a resounding victory.

The culmination of this impressive streak occurred in the third tournament, where S1mple elevated his game even further. His ability to stay calm under pressure and lead TBW in crucial situations was crucial in overcoming tough challenges. Spectators witnessed epic moments as S1mple, with his individual prowess, closed crucial rounds securing his team's victory.

S1mple and Team TBW's achievement not only solidify their position in CS:GO's elite but also leave an indelible mark in the game's history. Fans eagerly anticipate the future accomplishments of this talented team led by the Ukrainian legend.
