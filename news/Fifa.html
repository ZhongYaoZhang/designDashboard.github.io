FUT : 23 FUT LEVEL UPGRADE - EA ESPORTS Official Site

Take your players to the next level with special player items that will level up as they complete objectives.

Take the lead in the growth of your players with FUT LEVEL UP. Each player with FUT LEVEL UP has an upgraded version that you can unlock by completing objectives with their basic FUT LEVEL UP version. These upgrades can be rating boosters, filigree or bad leg and even premium chemistry upgrades, plus unique action shots that will help them stand out in your squad.

Discover the first release of FUT LEVEL UP player items in FIFA 23 Ultimate Team™. A second team will also be available on Friday, July 14. The basic FUT LEVEL UP player items will be among the player items for a limited time.

The FUT LEVEL UP campaign will also include FUT LEVEL UP themed content available in the objectives and Squad Building Challenges, among others, in FUT 23.
